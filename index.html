Our Model

Crisis-Intervention

  • TCI (milieu roles) — semiannual competencies
  • CPI (clinical/medical) — annual recert
  • Least-restrictive, trauma-informed care

Clinical & Family Care

  • Psychiatric evaluation & medication management
  • Individual, group, and family therapy
  • Education coordination & transition planning
